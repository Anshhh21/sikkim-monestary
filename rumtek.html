About Rumtek

Rumtek Monastery, also called Dharma Chakra Centre, is one of the most important monasteries in Sikkim. It is the seat of the Karmapa Lama, the head of the Karma Kagyu lineage of Tibetan Buddhism.

Built in the 16th century and reconstructed in the 20th century, the monastery is famous for its grand architecture, collection of sacred relics, and beautiful views of Gangtok.
