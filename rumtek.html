RUMTEK MONASTERY

Rumtek Monastery, also known as the Dharma Chakra Centre, is one of the most significant monasteries in Sikkim. It stands majestically on a hill facing Gangtok, representing the center of Kagyu Buddhism in India.

It houses rare Buddhist scriptures, art objects, and relics of the 16th Karmapa. The golden stupa and prayer wheels create an aura of peace that draws both pilgrims and tourists alike.
