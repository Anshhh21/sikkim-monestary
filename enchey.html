About Enchey

Enchey Monastery, situated on a hill above Gangtok, is more than 200 years old and belongs to the Nyingma order of Vajrayana Buddhism.

It is famous for the Chaam dance (masked dance) and its peaceful surroundings with views of Kanchenjunga.
